The Collegiate Wind Competition (CWC), hosted by the U.S. Department of Energy, challenges student teams from across the country to design, build, and present innovative wind energy solutions. It combines engineering, project development, and communication, giving students hands-on experience in renewable energy.

At NC State, Wolfpack Wind is proud to compete in this national event, developing real-world wind energy projects through collaboration across mechanical, electrical, and site development teams.
